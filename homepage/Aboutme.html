About Me

My name is Anaga Hettiarachchige and I am 16 years old as of now. I live in Queens, NY with my parents and my two sisters. I was born in Sri Lanka and I lived there for 6 years before moving to NYC 10 years ago. I have always put my education first as I love science and especially astronomy. I own a couple of online e-commerce businesses along with my other endeavours. I love playing basketball as I find basketball to be almost like therapy for the mind and body. My friends and I play basketball for hours on end at Telephone park no matter the weather. Along with basketball, I’ve been playing tennis and swimming all of my life. My dad being a national swimmer in Sri Lanka, has influenced a lot of my swimming career as well. I’ve also played in multiple tennis tournaments around Queens for an organization called NYJTL. Telephone park is the place where most of my childhood took place as it was where I met new friends, trained for tennis and basketball and where I could go no matter the weather or environment outside. It will become evident why Telephone park is so important to my life and why it represents such a large part of me being me in my essay!
